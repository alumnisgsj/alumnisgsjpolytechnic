About Us

Welcome to SGSJ Polytechnic Alumni Association

The SGSJ Polytechnic Alumni Association is a vibrant community of graduates dedicated to maintaining lifelong connections with their alma mater and fellow alumni. Since our establishment, we have been committed to fostering professional growth, networking opportunities, and giving back to the institution that shaped our careers.

Our alumni span across diverse industries and geographies, creating a powerful network of professionals who continue to uphold the values of excellence, innovation, and integrity instilled by SGSJ Polytechnic.

🎯 Our Mission

To connect, engage, and empower SGSJ Polytechnic alumni while supporting current students and contributing to the growth of our beloved institution.

👁️ Our Vision

To build a global alumni network that drives innovation, mentorship, and excellence, making SGSJ Polytechnic a beacon of technical education.

Why Join Us?

To connect, grow, and give back through a strong network of trusted peers.
